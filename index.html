What's New

  • New School Canteen

    The food options and the responsibilities to consider, canteens must be better orginized and more efficient than they have been in the past. Online menus with photograghs of the meals prepared and detailed descriptions of their ingredients help both students and parents make the right nutritional choices. Each student or staff member who accesses the canteen could be provided with a School Canteen Account on the school website that keeps track of their previous meal choices and any dietary requirements they might have. Ordering online by ticking a box and paying for meals online can eliminate all the fuss about money handling, making it safer and less complicated for the staff and students alike.
